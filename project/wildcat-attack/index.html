Project Wildcat Attack
Type Versus Fighting Game
Team 4 Students
Background Keimyung University (South Korea)
Role Project Manager, Game Designer & Developer
Inspiration Wildcat (Boston Dynamics Robot), Super Smash Bros
Language C#
Framework Unity
Tools 3D Studio Max
Design Pattern Game Loop, State Machine
Year 2014

Physics Based Versus Fighting

Wildcat Attack is a versus fighting game where two players have to push each other out of the stage.

To do so, each player has two directional buttons that will open or close front and back legs. When double tapping a direction their robot will dash. This dash will make their robot heat rise and if it fills its heat gauge (bottom of the screen) it will be unable to dash for a period of time.

The dash being a powerful defensive and offensive feature, the difficulty lies in the heat management. When overheat the player will have no choice but to try to avoid attacks by jumping.

The gameplay relies on Unity's physics engine and is wobbly by design.
